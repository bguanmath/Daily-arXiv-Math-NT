### [1] [Quasiautomorphic forms are isomorphic to vector-valued automorphic forms](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21067)
*Michael Andrew Henry*

Main category: math.NT

TL;DR: 文章利用Hecke三角形群的拟自守形式定义从拟自守形式到向量值自守形式的映射，提出Hecke向量形式的概念，并证明其函数方程和双射性质，该结果对拟模形式也成立。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 建立拟自守形式与向量值自守形式之间的对应关系，以统一的框架处理Hecke三角形群上的函数方程问题。

Method: 利用Hecke三角形群的结构定义映射，通过乘子系统证明函数方程和双射性质。

Result: 证明了Hecke向量形式在群生成元下的函数方程，并完成从拟自守形式到向量值自守形式的双射证明。

Conclusion: 由于模群是Hecke三角形群的特殊情况，该结果可直接推广到拟模形式情形。

Abstract: We utilize the structure of quasiautomorphic forms over a Hecke triangle group to define a mapping from a quasiautomorphic form to a vector-valued automorphic form (vvaf). This kind of vvaf we call a Hecke vector-form. First we supply a proof of the functional equations that hold for Hecke vector-forms modulo the group generators. Then, utilizing the multiplier system for these Hecke vector-forms, we prove the opposite direction and complete the bijection. Since the modular group is a special instance of the Hecke triangle groups, our results hold for quasimodular forms.

### [2] [On a conjecture of Amdeberhan, Andrews and Ballantine for double Lambert series](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21163)
*Rahul Kumar,Aman Singh*

Main category: math.NT

TL;DR: 本文证明了Amdeberhan、Andrews和Ballantine提出的一个关于双Lambert级数的猜想，即系数q^{N2^a}的特定双Lambert级数等于σ_1(N)，即N的所有正因数之和。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 证明一个关于双Lambert级数的未解决猜想，将系数与经典的除数函数联系起来。该猜想最初由Amdeberhan、Andrews和Ballantine提出，涉及复杂的q-级数展开。

Method: 主要方法是将左边的双Lambert级数转化为单和，从而推导出准模形式E_2(q)的新表示。通过精心设计的变换和级数操作，将双重求和简化。

Result: 成功证明了原猜想成立，即对于特定的a值，系数coeff{q^{N2^a}}的表达式等于σ_1(N)。同时获得了准模形式E_2(q)的新表示。

Conclusion: 该工作不仅解决了原猜想，还建立了双Lambert级数与准模形式之间的联系，为q-级数理论提供了新的见解和方法。

Abstract: In this note, we prove a recent conjecture of Amdeberhan, Andrews and Ballantine concerning a double Lambert series. More precisely, they conjectured that \[ \coeff{q^{N2^a}} \sum_{m,k\geq 1} \frac{q^{mk2^a}}{(1+q^{k2^{a-1}})(1-q^{2m-1})} =σ_1(N), \] where $σ_1(N)$ is the sum of all the positive divisors of $N$. The main idea of the proof is to first transform a double Lambert series on the left-hand side into a single sum. This leads us to derive a new representation of quasi-modular forms $E_2(q)$.

### [3] [Binomial coefficients with divisors avoiding an interval](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21221)
*Hung M. Bui,Kyle Pratt,Alexandru Zaharescu*

Main category: math.NT

TL;DR: 本文研究了Erdős和Graham关于二项式系数的一个有五十年历史的猜想，证明了当k足够大时猜想成立，但在GRH假设下存在反例，从而在GRH下解决了该猜想。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Erdős和Graham在约五十年前提出猜想：对于任意1≤k≤n/2，二项式系数(n choose k)是否必定有一个≤n的因子「接近」n（即大于常数倍n）？这个问题涉及数论中二项式系数的因子结构。

Method: 当k足够大时使用直接分析方法；对于k较小的情况，证明涉及受限覆盖问题（residue classes的覆盖）、筛法（sieve methods）以及各种指数和估计（exponential sum estimates）。

Result: 证明了当k相对于n足够大时，该猜想成立。然而在GRH（广义黎曼猜想）假设下，找到了k相对于n较小时的反例，即(n choose k)不存在≤n且接近n的因子。

Conclusion: 在GRH假设下彻底解决了Erdős-Graham猜想：猜想在k较大时成立，但存在反例表明不能推广到所有k的情况。

Abstract: We investigate a fifty-year-old conjecture of Erdős and Graham concerning whether the binomial coefficient ${n \choose k}$ with $1 \leq k \leq \frac{n}{2}$ must always have a divisor $\leq n$ that is ``close'' to $n$: that is, bigger than a constant times $n$. We show this is the case when $k$ is sufficiently large as a function of $n$. However, we show (under the Generalized Riemann Hypothesis) it is possible to find binomial coefficients ${n \choose k}$, where $k$ is small compared to $n$, such that ${n \choose k}$ does not have divisors $\leq n$ close to $n$. This settles the conjecture of Erdős and Graham, under GRH. This latter, more substantial argument involves a restricted covering problem with residue classes, sieve methods, and various exponential sum estimates.

</details>

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 改进射线原点常数δ的最佳已知上界，这是Dirichlet谱研究中的重要问题。

Method: 基于V. A. Ivanov的方法进行改进，构造了由偏商限制定义的Cantor型集F_4^*，证明了其厚度τ(log(F_4^*))>1，并应用Cantor集合的和集结果证明F_4^*·F_4^*是区间。

Result: 获得了新的上界δ≤(111(397+√26565))/65522≈0.94866。

Abstract: We study the continuous part of the Dirichlet spectrum $\mathbb{D}$ and improve the best previously published upper bound for the ray-origin constant $δ$. Building on and refining V. A. For its thickness, we prove $τ(\log(F_4^*))>1$, and apply sum-set results for Cantor sets to prove that the set $F_4^* \cdot F_4^*$ is an interval. Finally, we establish a new upper bound $δ\le \frac{111(397+\sqrt{26565})}{65522}\approx0.94866$.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 本文采用三种主要方法：(1) 生成函数操作，通过构造和操作适当的生成函数来分析分区函数的性质；(2) Hecke特征形式理论，利用Hecke算子的特征形式性质；(3) Newman的结果，即关于分区同余的已知定理和结果。

Conclusion: 通过生成函数操作、Hecke特征形式理论和Newman结果的综合应用，成功建立了分区函数$\bar{a}_s(n)$在模2幂意义下的无限族同余性质，丰富了分区同余理论。

Abstract: Let $\bar{a}_s(n)$ denote the number of partitions of $n$, wherein each odd part is multicolored (atmost $s\ge 1$ colors) and the first appearance of parts may be overlined. Our approach builds upon generating function manipulations, Hecke eigenform theory and results of Newman.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 改进现有的除数问题和圆问题的Ω结果。这两个问题是解析数论中的重要问题，与狄利克雷除数问题和高斯圆问题的误差项相关。

Method: 主要新方法是一种共振方法，直接处理Voronoï求和公式中出现的相位。该方法使用单边扇形核（伽马分布的密度）代替通常的正核，其傅里叶变换位于复平面的适当扇区中。

Result: 建立了在猜想上尖锐的Ω结果，并确定了大值的符号。这是首次对Hafner 1981年除数问题的Ω+结果和圆问题的Ω-结果进行改进。

Abstract: We establish omega results for the divisor and circle problems that are conjecturally sharp, while also determining the sign of the large values obtained. This improves on the work of Soundararajan and on the subsequent independent refinements of Sourmelidis and Mahatab, and gives the first improvement on Hafner's 1981 $Ω_+$ result for the divisor problem and his $Ω_-$ result for the circle problem. The main new ingredient is a resonance method which works directly with the phase appearing in the Voronoï summation formula. This is achieved by replacing the usual positive kernels by a one-sided sectorial kernel, namely the density of a Gamma distribution, whose Fourier transform lies in a suitable sector of the complex plane.

*Iacopo Nonis*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文研究reduced 0-Auslander extriangulated范畴，引入了(signed) presilting序列的概念，建立了其与(signed) τ-exceptional序列之间的双射，并定义了新的τ-cluster morphism范畴。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 受Pan-Zhu对这类范畴的silting约化的启发，旨在提供Buan-Marsh双射的新视角，并建立extriangulated范畴与代数表示论之间的联系。

Method: 引入(signed) presilting序列概念，利用P（范畴C的投射生成元）构造Λ=End_C(P)，建立presilting序列与τ-exceptional序列之间的双射；定义τ-cluster morphism范畴M(C)，其对象为某些extension-closed子范畴，态射由signed presilting序列描述。

Result: 建立了(signed) presilting序列与(signed) τ-exceptional序列之间的双射，这为Buan-Marsh双射（signed τ-exceptional序列与有序support τ-rigid对象）提供了新视角；从M(C)可恢复Λ的τ-cluster morphism范畴。

Conclusion: 该研究统一了extriangulated范畴与代数表示论中τ-例外序列的理论，推广了现有的双射结果，并建立了新的τ-cluster morphism范畴作为连接这两大领域的桥梁。

Abstract: Let $\mathscr{C}$ be a reduced $0$-Auslander extriangulated category. This correspondence provides a new perspective on the Buan--Marsh bijection between signed $τ$-exceptional sequences and ordered support $τ$-rigid objects. Furthermore, we introduce a new category $\mathfrak{M}(\mathscr{C})$, called the $τ$-cluster morphism category of $\mathscr{C}$, whose objects are certain extension-closed subcategories of $\mathscr{C}$ and whose morphisms are described in terms of signed presilting sequences. As an application, we recover the $τ$-cluster morphism category of $Λ$ from $\mathfrak{M}(\mathscr{C})$.
