### [1] [Weighted sums of Lucas numbers of the first and second kind](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.10797)
*aBa Mbirika*

Main category: math.NT

TL;DR: 将C. R. Wall的广义斐波那契加权和公式推广到第一类、第二类Lucas序列，并提供8种著名数列的新加权和恒等式。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Wall提出的广义斐波那契加权和公式存在局限性，需扩展至更广的Lucas序列类别，并为常见特殊数列建立统一恒等式框架。

Method: 使用Abel分部求和方法推导第一类Lucas序列 $
\sum i U_i$
 和第二类 $
\sum i V_i$
 的闭式解，具体应用时代入8种特殊数列参数。

Result: 获得$
\sum i U_i$
 和$
\sum i V_i$
 的通用闭式表达式，成功恢复斐波那契/卢卡斯数列加权和，并首次给出Pell、Jacobsathl等8种数列的新加权和恒等式。

Conclusion: Abel求和法有效统一了Lucas序列的加权和问题，所建框架可生成无穷多具体恒等式，显著扩展了组合数论中的加权和工具箱。

Abstract: In the \textit{Fibonacci Quarterly} in 1964, C.~R.~Wall gave the following weighted sum of generalized Fibonacci numbers: $\sum_{i=1}^n i G_i = n G_{n+2} - G_{n+3} + G_3$, where $\left(G_n\right)_{n \geq 0}$ is defined by the recurrence $G_n = G_{n-1} + G_{n-2}$ with fixed $G_0, G_1 \in \mathbb{Z}$. In this paper, we generalize Wall's result to the Lucas sequences of the first and second kind, $\left(U_n(p,q)\right)_{n \geq 0}$ and $\left(V_n(p,q)\right)_{n \geq 0}$, and give closed forms for $\sum_{i=1}^n i U_i$ and $\sum_{i=1}^n i V_i$ by using Abel's summation by parts method. Moreover, we provide concrete applications, not only recovering the known weighted sums $\sum_{i=1}^n i F_i$ and $\sum_{i=1}^n i L_i$ of Fibonacci and Lucas numbers, respectively, but also add new identities to the literature for eight well-known sequences. In particular, we give closed forms for weighted sums of Lucas sequences of the first kind such as the Pell, balancing, Jacobstahl, and Mersenne numbers, and also Lucas sequences of the second kind such as the companion Pell, double Lucas-balancing, Jacobstahl-Lucas, and Mersenne-Lucas numbers.

### [3] [Trito-non-ordinary Iwasawa theory of diagonal cycles](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.11511)
*Raúl Alonso,Kâzım Büyükboduk,Antonio Cauchi,Antonio Lei*

Main category: math.NT

TL;DR: 该论文针对一个特殊的三元积形式引入了Euler系统，并研究其Iwasawa理论。当基域为分叉四元数代数时，通过CM性质构造了符号反循环分支，提出了带符号的Perrin-Riou型Iwasawa主猜想，并部分证明了该猜想。此外，将Hsieh的平衡三元p进L函数推广到半非普通情形。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在分叉四元数代数上建立半非普通三元积（含一个p-普通、一个非普通特征形式和一个Hida族）的Euler系统理论，为反循环Iwasawa理论提供新工具，并构造带符号的p进L函数。

Method: 1. 定义带符号的对角循环Euler系统
2. 当基域为M_2(Q)且Hida族具有CM时，发展带符号的反循环Iwasawa理论
3. 提出并部分证明带符号的Perrin-Riou型Iwasawa主猜想
4. 扩展Hsieh的平衡三元p进L函数构造到半非普通场景

Result: 1. 建立了半非普通三元积的Euler系统理论框架
2. 在特定CM情形下实现了符号反循环Iwasawa理论
3. 证明了Iwasawa主猜想的一个包含关系
4. 构造了半非普通场景的带符号三元p进L函数

Conclusion: 该研究将Euler系统和Iwasawa理论推广至半非普通三元积情形，为p进Langlands纲领提供新工具。部分证明的Iwasawa主猜想及构造的p进L函数有望在BSD猜想等领域发挥作用。

Abstract: Our goal in this paper is to introduce and study the Euler system of signed diagonal cycles associated with a trito-non-ordinary triple product of the form $f^B \times g^B \times \mathbf{h}^B$, where $f^B$ (resp. $g^B$) is a $p$-ordinary (resp. non-ordinary) eigenform on an indefinite quaternion algebra $B_{/\mathbb{Q}}$ of weight $2$, and $\mathbf{h}^B$ is a primitive Hida ($p$-ordinary) family. When $B=\mathrm{M}_2(\mathbb{Q})$ is split and $\mathbf{h}=\mathbf{h}^B$ has CM by an imaginary quadratic field, this allows us to develop the signed anticyclotomic Iwasawa theory for the base change $\mathrm{BC}_{K/\mathbb{Q}}(π_f)\times \mathrm{BC}_{K/\mathbb{Q}}(π_g)\times ψ$, where $ψ$ is a Hecke character of $K$. We formulate a signed Perrin-Riou-style Iwasawa main conjecture in this setting, and obtain a result on one inclusion in this conjecture. Our methods also allow us to extend Hsieh's construction of the balanced triple-product $p$-adic $L$-function to the trito-non-ordinary scenario, and to define its signed counterparts.

### [4] [Uniform bounds and uncertainty for asymptotics of representations of $p$-adic ${\rm GL}_N$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.11063)
*Rahul Dalal,Mathilde Gerbelli-Gauthier,Simon Marshall*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文研究p进GL_N表示的固定向量在主同余子群下的维数增长，证明两个结果：一是关于固定向量增长的统一界，扩展到Harish-Chandra-Howe系数的统一界；二是对于酉表示，GK维数与矩阵系数衰减速率之间的定量关系。两个结果相互独立，基于Langlands和Zelevinsky分类框架证明。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探索p进一般线性群表示下固定向量维数增长规律，旨在建立与表示几何结构的定量联系，填补现有理论在统一边界和衰减速率定量描述方面的空白。

Method: 采用Langlands和Zelevinsky分类理论框架，通过代数表示论方法分析主同余子群作用下的表示空间结构。第一个结果通过GK维数控制固定向量增长；第二个结果对酉表示建立GK维数与矩阵系数衰减的显式关系式。

Result: 1. 获得独立于表示选择的固定向量维数增长统一上界，该界由GK维数决定，并推广到Harish-Chandra-Howe系数；2. 证明酉表示GK维数与矩阵系数衰减速率的定量对应关系。

Conclusion: 解决了p进群表示理论中两个关键问题：固定向量增长的普适性控制及酉表示几何性质与解析性质的定量关联，为后续研究提供新的分析工具。

Abstract: We prove two results on the growth of dimensions of fixed vectors of representations $π$ of $p$-adic ${\rm GL}_N$ under principal congruence subgroups: First, a uniform bound on the growth of fixed vectors in terms of the GK-dimension $π$, which we extend to a uniform bound on the Harish-Chandra--Howe coefficients. Second, for $π$ unitary, a quantitative relationship between the GK-dimension of $π$ and the rate of decay of its matrix coefficients. These results are independent of one another and proved in the framework of the Langlands and Zelevinsky classifications.

### [5] [Estimating spectral radius of Bell-type operator via finite dimensional approximation of orthogonal projections](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.10939)
*Yuki Fujii,Toyohiro Tsurumaru*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 这篇论文研究了量子力学中两个正交投影P和Q的交换子[A,B]的谱半径估计问题，其中A=2P-I，B=2Q-I。该问题与贝尔-CHSH算子的谱半径确定问题等价。论文提出了通过有限维子空间上的正交投影来近似的方法，并提供了谱半径的上下界，且在矩阵表示为'常角单移形式'时达到精确值。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在量子力学中，估计正交投影P和Q的交换子[A,B]（其中A=2P-I，B=2Q-I）的谱半径是一个重要问题，这与贝尔-CHSH算子的谱半径确定问题等价。为解决此问题，需要开发有效的近似方法。

Method: 提出一种方法，通过有限维子空间上的正交投影来近似无限维希尔伯特空间V上的正交投影。该方法计算谱半径的上下界，并证明当投影P和Q的矩阵表示为'常角单移形式'时，上下界相等，从而得到精确值。

Result: 该方法提供了交换子[A,B]谱半径的上界和下界。在投影的矩阵表示为常角单移形式时，上下界一致，给出了谱半径的精确值。

Conclusion: 通过有限维逼近，论文为无限维空间中正交投影交换子的谱半径提供了可计算的估计方法，并在特定矩阵形式下得到精确解，对量子力学中的贝尔-CHSH算子问题有直接应用价值。

Abstract: In quantum mechanics, the following problem is important: For two orthogonal projections P and Q on a separable infinite dimensional Hilbert space V, estimate the spectral radius of the commutator [A,B], where A=2P-I, B=2Q-I. This problem is known to be equivalent to determining the spectral radius of the Bell-CHSH operator.
  To approach this problem, we give a method to approximate orthogonal projections on V by those on finite dimensional subspaces.
  This method provides an upper bound and a lower bound for the spectral radius of [A,B], which become exact when the matrix representations of P and Q are "constant-angle one-shifted form".

</details>
