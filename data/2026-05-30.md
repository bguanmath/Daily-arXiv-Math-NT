### [1] [Reduction of Symmetry in Quaternionic Analysis and Invariant Trilinear Forms](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29094)
*Igor Frenkel,Matvei Libine*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文研究四元数分析中正规函数和双重正规函数表示空间之间的不变三重线性形式。通过将保角群SU(2,2)的对称性约化到辛群Sp(4,R)，构建了这种不变量，并揭示了这些函数空间在辛群限制下的不可约性和由双曲覆盖生成的复合结构。

Motivation: 构建不变量三重线性形式有两个动机：一是证明正规函数和双重正规函数表示空间之间不存在不变量(除了平凡情况)；二是将四元数分析建立在更基本的辛对称性基础上，这有助于研究Maxwell方程的spinor表示。

Method: 通过将保角群SU(2,2)(或SL(4,C)的实形式)的对称性约化到辛群Sp(4,R)，利用Sp(4,R)的双重 cover —— metaplectic群的表示来分析函数空间的构造，并在此框架下构建不变三重线性形式。

Result: 证明了正规函数和双重正规函数之间不存在Sp(4,R)不变的三重线性形式；但在辛群框架下，成功构建了双重正规函数之间的不变量，以及正规函数和准正规函数之间的非平凡配对。

Conclusion: 辛群Sp(4,R)是研究四元数分析的一个更基础的对称性群。在此框架下，函数空间具有由metaplectic表示产生的复合结构，这为研究基于双重正规函数的spinor表示及Maxwell方程铺平了道路。

Abstract: In our previous papers we repeatedly emphasized the special role in Quaternionic Analysis of the conformal group SU(2,2) and other real forms of its complexification SL(4,C). In particular, the natural product map of the left and right regular functions into a larger representation that contains the doubly regular functions as a subquotient is an intertwining operator. In this paper we show, however, that the spaces of regular and doubly regular functions do not "interact" - there is no invariant trilinear form on the tensor product of these representations.
  To construct a natural invariant trilinear form, we reduce the conformal group symmetry to the symplectic subgroup Sp(4,R). This suggests a new approach to the Quaternionic Analysis in general, and we make the first steps in this paper. It turns out that the spaces of regular and doubly regular functions are still irreducible after the restriction to the symplectic subgroup and have a composed structure arising from the metaplectic representation of the double cover of Sp(4,R) - the metaplectic group. This also leads us to consider the double covers of the quaternionic spaces and non-trivial pairings between them. Our study of Quaternionic Analysis based on the symplectic symmetry group culminates in the construction of the invariant trilinear forms on the products of spaces of doubly regular functions and certain counterparts of regular and quasi regular functions.
  Additional motivation for constructing invariant trilinear forms comes from their application to spinor representations of certain quaternionic algebras based on the doubly regular functions. The latter can be viewed as the space of solutions of the Maxwell equation, and their spinor representations are of a great importance to quantum field theory. These spinor representations will be the subject of a forthcoming paper.
