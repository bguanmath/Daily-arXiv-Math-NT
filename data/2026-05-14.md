### [1] [On quandle representations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.12692)
*Mohamad Maassarani*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 论文研究了有限维 quandle 表示的可分解性，给出了可分解为不可约表示直和的充要条件，并探讨了不可约表示的酉性条件。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 并非所有的 quandle 表示都能分解为不可约表示的直和，作者希望找出能够进行这种分解的条件，并研究不可约表示的酉性质。

Method: 利用线性代数方法，特别是关于矩阵可对角化的性质，以及酉表示与行列式模的关系。

Result: 证明了有限维 quandle 表示可分解为不可约直和当且仅当像中每个元素都可对角化；不可约表示在某内积下是酉的当且仅当像中每个元素的行列式模为1；任何不可约表示可通过 twist 获得酉不可约表示；有限 quandle 的包络群能够实现忠实的有限维酉表示；有限 quandle 的不可约表示是一维的当且仅当包络群是阿贝尔的。

Conclusion: 建立了有限维 quandle 表示的可分解理论，刻画了酉不可约表示的特征，并确定了一族有限 quandle 的不可约表示。

Abstract: A unitary finite dimensional quandle representation is decomposable into a direct sum of irreducible represenations. Not all quandle representations satisfy this property. We prove that a finite dimensional quandle represenation $ρ:Q \to GL(V) $ of a finite quandle $Q$ over $\mathbb{C}$ is decomposable into a direct sum of irreducibles if and only if every element in the image of $ρ$ is diagonlizable. We show that an irreducible representation $ρ:Q \to GL(V)$ of a finite quandle over $\mathbb{C}$ is unitary for some inner product if and only if every element of the image of $ρ$ has determinant of modulus $1$. It follows that any irreducible representation of a finite quandle $Q$ over $\mathbb{C}$ can be twisted by a quandle character to obtain a unitary irreducible representation. We also prove that the enveloping group $G(Q)$, of a finite quandle $Q$, admit a faithfull finite dimensional unitary representation over $\mathbb{C}$ and that the irreducible representations of a finite quandle $Q$ over $\mathbb{C}$ are $1$-dimensional if and only if $G(Q)$ is abelian. Finaly, we determine the irreducible representations over $\mathbb{C}$ of a family of finite quandles.

### [2] [On the additivity of projective presentations of maximal rank](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13029)
*Grzegorz Bobiński,Jan Schröer*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 研究有限维代数上有限维模的投射表示，特别是τ-正则模（τ-regular modules）的投射表示的最大秩行为。研究是否最大秩投射表示具有加性，以及τ-正则模何时可以约化到投射维数不超过1的模。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: τ-正则模是投射维数不超过1的模和τ-刚模的推广。研究动机是探索这类模的性质，特别是最大秩投射表示的加性问题，以及τ-正则模能否约化到更简单的模类。

Method: 使用表示论和模论方法，研究模簇（module varieties）的几何性质，分析τ-正则模形成的开集及其闭包的不可约分支。

Result: τ-正则模形成模簇的开集，其闭包是所谓的生成τ-正则（generically τ-正则）不可约分支。当τ-正则模或生成τ-正则分支可以约化到投射维数不超过1的模或分支时，这与最大秩表示的加性问题密切相关。

Conclusion: 最大秩投射表示的加性与τ-正则模能否约化到投射维数不超过1的模之间存在密切联系。

Abstract: We study projective presentations of finite-dimensional modules over finite-dimensional algebras. We discuss if projective presentations of maximal rank behave additively. More precisely, we ask if the direct sum of copies of a projective presentation of maximal rank is again of maximal rank. The modules which have a projective presentation of maximal rank are exactly the $τ$-regular modules. This class of modules can be seen as a generalization of modules of projective dimension at most one, and of $τ$-rigid modules. The $τ$-regular modules form open subsets of module varieties. Their closures are therefore unions of irreducible components, which are called generically $τ$-regular. We discuss when a $τ$-regular module or a generically $τ$-regular component can be reduced to a module or component of projective dimension at most one, and we show that this is closely related to the question on the additivity of maximal rank presentations.
