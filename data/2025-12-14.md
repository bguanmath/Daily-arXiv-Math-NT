*Wei Wang*

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究划分函数中最⼩部分的计数函数spt(n)的算术性质，探索其与模形式理论的联系，特别是如何通过赫克迹和扭曲⼆次狄利克雷级数来描述spt函数的递归关系和模性质。

Method: 使⽤赫克迹技术和扭曲⼆次狄利克雷级数，推导spt函数的广义欧拉式递归公式，并⽤权为ℓ+1的尖形式在SL2(Z)上的赫克迹来表示模ℓ下的幂级数。

Abstract: Let $\operatorname{spt}(n)$ be the number of smallest parts in the partitions of $n$. In this paper, we give some generalized Euler-like recursive formulas for the $\operatorname{spt}$ function in terms of Hecke trace of values of special twisted quadratic Dirichlet series. As a corollary, we give a closed form expressions of the power series $\sum_{n\geq 0}\operatorname{spt}(\ell n-δ_{\ell})q^n\pmod{\ell}$, $δ_{\ell}:=(\ell^2-1)/24$, by Hecke traces for weight $\ell+1 $ cusp forms on $\operatorname{SL}_2(\mathbb{Z})$. We further establish an incongruence result for the $\operatorname{spt}$ function.

### [2] [A purity theorem for Mahler equations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.10661)
*Colin Faverjon,Julien Roques*

Main category: math.NT

TL;DR: 本文建立了马勒函数的纯粹性定理，类似于Chudnovsky兄弟关于G-函数的著名纯粹性定理。方法基于对马勒方程解的深入研究，特别是广义马勒级数的构造。结果表明，广义马勒级数在分类的五层过滤中，属于最大的三个层次的性质会在其最小马勒方程的所有解中传播。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 建立马勒函数的纯粹性定理，类比已有的G-函数和E-函数的纯粹性定理，深化对马勒方程解的结构理解。

Method: 通过马勒系统的约化研究，证明任何马勒方程都有由广义马勒级数构成的完整解基。使用Adamczewski、Bell和Smertnig的高度间隙定理，建立广义马勒级数的算术增长自然过滤。

Result: 纯粹性定理表明，广义马勒级数若属于五层过滤中最大的三个层次，则该性质会在其最小马勒方程的所有解中传播。同时证明了该定理不能推广到最小的两个层次。

Conclusion: 建立了马勒函数的纯粹性定理，揭示了广义马勒级数解的算术增长性质在解空间中的传播规律，完善了对马勒方程解的结构理解。

Abstract: The principal aim of this paper is to establish a purity theorem for Mahler functions that is reminiscent of famous purity theorems for G-functions by D. and G. Chudnovsky and for E-functions (and, more generally, for holonomic arithmetic Gevrey series) by Y. Andr{é}. Our approach is based on a preliminary study of independent interest of the nature of the solutions of Mahler equations. Roughly speaking, we prove a reduction result for Mahler systems, implying that any Mahler equation admits a complete basis of solutions formed of what we call generalized Mahler series. These are sums involving Puiseux series, Hahn series of a very special type and solutions of inhomogeneous equations of order 1 with constant coefficients. In the light of B. Adamczewski, J. P. Bell and D. Smertnig's recent height gap theorem, we introduce a natural filtration on the set of generalized Mahler series according to the arithmetic growth of the coefficients of the Puiseux series involved in their decomposition. This filtration has five pieces. Our purity theorem states that the membership of a generalized Mahler series to one of the three largest pieces of this filtration propagates to any other generalized Mahler series solution of its minimal Mahler equation. We also show that this statement does not extend to the smallest two pieces.

### [3] [Revisiting the equation $x^2+y^3=z^p$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.10781)

Main category: math.NT

TL;DR: 本文研究了与椭圆曲线相关的模曲线X_E^-(p)的局部有理点存在性，并将其应用于费马型方程x^2 + y^3 = z^p的解的分类问题。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 利用模方法研究费马型方程x^2 + y^3 = z^p的解析构，通过分析相关模曲线X_E^-(p)的局部有理点存在性来排除某些解的可能性。

Method: 通过Freitas--Naskręcki--Stoll的工作将方程解与模曲线X_E^-(p)的有理点关联，然后应用作者开发的局部点存在性判定准则分析模曲线在局部域上的点存在性。

Result: 对于导数为864的椭圆曲线和满足p ≡ 19 mod 24的特定素数，证明了X_E^-(p)在局部域上没有有理点；对于列表中所有椭圆曲线和任意素数，展示了这些曲线要么可用局部准则排除，要么无法仅用局部信息排除。

Conclusion: 局部信息分析为研究费马型方程的模方法提供了有力工具，但对某些曲线需要更全局的方法来完全解决问题。

Abstract: Let $E/\mathbb Q$ be an elliptic curve and $p \geq 3$ a prime. The modular curve $X_E^-(p)$ parameterizes elliptic curves with $p$-torsion modules anti-symplectically isomorphic to $E[p]$. The work of Freitas--Naskręcki--Stoll uses the modular method to show that all primitive non-trivial solutions of the Fermat-type equation $x^2 + y^3 = z^p$ give rise to rational points on $X_E^-(p)$ with $E \in \{27a1,54a1,96a1,288a1,864a1,864b1,864c1 \}$. Using a criterion classifying the existence of local points due to the first two authors, we show that, for $E$ any of the curves with conductor 864 and certain primes $p \equiv 19 \pmod{24}$, we have $X_E^-(p)(\mathbb Q_\ell) = \emptyset$. Furthermore, for each $E$ in the list and any $p$, we prove that either $X_E^-(p)$ can be discarded using the same criterion, or it cannot be discarded using purely local information.
