Result: 本文得到了两个主要结果：1）强化了Chang引理，证明大谱所在子空间外的字符在陪集上平均也有小的相关性；2）作为一个应用，建立了局部化计数引理；3）将论证方法推广到任意有限阿贝尔群。

Conclusion: 本文的成果为加性组合学提供了更精细的分析工具，强化后的Chang引理及其推论在处理谱结构和平滑性问题时具有更强的适用性。

Abstract: We prove a strengthening of Chang's lemma for subsets of $\mathbb F_p^n$. The classical conclusion that the large spectrum is contained in a subspace of dimension at most $2\varepsilon^{-2}\log(1/α)$ is refined to show that every character outside this subspace has small correlation with the set not only globally, but also on average over the cosets of the orthogonal complement, in a natural cosetwise $\ell^1$ norm. As a consequence, we obtain a localized counting lemma. We also give an extension of the argument to arbitrary finite abelian groups.
