### [1] [On the p-part of the conductor of a generalised character](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01351)
*Markus Linckelmann*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文证明了有限群广义特征的p部分指数与其广义分解数的指数相等，并用于证明在具有自同态源的Morita型稳定等价中，由同伦和完全等距保持的不可约特征的p部分指数。应用于具有阿贝尔缺陷和弗罗贝尼乌斯惯性商的分块。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究有限群特征理论中指数与广义分解数的关系，特别是在具有自同态源的Morita型稳定等价背景下的不变性问题。

Method: 证明广义特征的p部分指数等于其广义分解数的指数，并利用这一结果分析在特定等价关系下p部分指数的保持性质。

Result: 成功证明了p部分指数的等式成立，并建立了在具有阿贝尔缺陷和弗罗贝尼乌斯惯性商的分块中相关不变性。

Conclusion: 该结果深化了对有限群特征理论中指数性质的理解，为研究稳定等价下的特征不变性提供了新的工具和视角。

Abstract: We show that the $p$-part of the conductor of a generalised character of a finite group is equal to the conductor of its generalised decomposition numbers. We use this to show that $p$-parts of conductors of irreducible characters are preserved under isotypies and perfect isometries that arise in the context of stable equivalences of Morita type with endopermutation source.
  We apply this to blocks with abelian defect and Frobenius inertial quotient.

### [2] [On the number of irreducible representations for finite unipotent Heisenberg-type groups](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01358)
*Mikhail Ignatev,Leonid Titov*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 文章研究了某些推广海森堡群的不可约表示数量，发现其是(q-1)的非负整数系数多项式，验证了Isaacs猜想。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究代数群表示理论中的轨道方法，探索推广海森堡群（包括广义海森堡群和某些经典正交代数群极大幂幺子群）的表示数量计算问题。

Method: 使用轨道方法，将$U$的不可约表示分类与余伴随轨道联系起来，针对广义海森堡群（由任意双线性形式定义）和正交代数群中的特定子群进行表示数量计算。

Result: 计算得到的不可约表示数量是$q-1$的非负整数系数多项式，这一结果与Isaacs猜想一致。

Conclusion: 成功计算了两类推广海森堡群的不可约表示数量，验证了其多项式表达式性质，支持了Isaacs猜想。

Abstract: Let $U$ be an algebraic subgroup of the group of $n\times n$ upper-triangular matrices with units on the diagonal over a finite field of large enough characteristic, and $\mathfrak{n}$ be the Lie algebra of $U$. The main tool in representation theory of $U$ is the orbit method, which classifies irreducible representations of the group $U$ in terms of coadjoint orbits on the dual space $\mathfrak{n}^*$. We consider two types of generalizations of the Heisenberg group, namely, generalized Heisenberg groups defined with an arbitrary bilinear form, and certain subgroups in maximal unipotent subgroups of classical orthogonal algebraic groups. We provide a way to calculate the number of irreducible representations of such groups. It turned out that this number is a polynomial in $q-1$ with nonnegative integer coefficients, which agrees with Isaacs' conjecture.

</details>


### [3] [A Betti geometric Casselman-Shalika equivalence](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01423)
*Colton Sandvik*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文研究Betti层中的Kirillov模型，发展仿射格拉斯曼流形上的Iwahori-Whittaker层理论，并证明了Betti几何的Casselman-Shalika等价关系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Whittaker层在几何表示论中广泛应用，但其定义限制在étale层或D-模上。需要为更一般的层理论（如Betti层）建立相应的理论框架。

Method: 采用Gaitsgory和Lysenko提出的Kirillov模型，在Betti层设置中发展仿射格拉斯曼流形上的Iwahori-Whittaker层理论。

Result: 建立了Betti几何的Casselman-Shalika等价关系，将仿射格拉斯曼流形上的Iwahori-Whittaker交错层与Satake范畴联系起来。

Conclusion: 通过Kirillov模型成功拓展了Whittaker层理论到Betti层框架，为几何表示论提供了新的工具和等价关系。

Abstract: Whittaker sheaves are ubiquitous in geometric representation theory; however, their definition requires one to restrict the sheaf-theoretic setting to either étale sheaves or $D$-modules. Gaitsgory and Lysenko proposed a solution to this problem called the Kirillov model, which is well-defined for many sheaf theories. In this paper, we advance the study of the Kirillov model in the setting of Betti sheaves with a particular emphasis on developing a theory of Iwahori-Whittaker sheaves on the affine Grassmannian. Using this framework, we prove a Betti geometric Casselman-Shalika equivalence, which relates Iwahori-Whittaker perverse sheaves on the affine Grassmannian with the Satake category.

</details>


### [4] [Nilpotent orbits of classical Lie algebras stable under negation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01427)
*Guillaume Neuttiens,Jérémie Pierard de Maujouy*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文研究了经典李代数幂零伴随轨道上Gibbs态的存在性问题。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Gibbs态是定义在Hamiltonian G-流形上的概率分布，自然参数化为李代数g的元素。作者关注最简单的Hamiltonian G-流形特例——李代数的余伴随轨道。

Method: 重点研究了经典李代数的幂零余伴随轨道（等价于幂零伴随轨道）。分析了在乘以-1下保持稳定的幂零轨道上Gibbs态的存在性。

Result: 证明在乘以-1下保持稳定的幂零轨道上Gibbs态不存在，并对所有经典李代数的情况进行了完全分类。

Conclusion: 该研究为经典李代数幂零轨道上Gibbs态的存在性提供了完整的分类结果，揭示了在这些特定轨道上Gibbs态的结构性限制。

Abstract: Gibbs states are probability distributions defined on Hamiltonian G-manifolds that are naturally parametrized by elements of the Lie algebra g. In this paper, we focus on a specific case of the simplest Hamiltonian G-manifolds, the coadjoint orbits of Lie algebras. We look at the nilpotent coadjoint orbits of the classical Lie algebras, or equivalently the nilpotent adjoint orbits. We show that Gibbs states do not exist on nilpotent orbits that are stable under multiplication by -1, and proceed to classify those for all classical Lie algebras.

</details>
