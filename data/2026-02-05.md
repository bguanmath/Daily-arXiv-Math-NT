### [7] [Multivariate and quantitative Erdős-Kac laws for Beatty sequences](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.04875)
*Fredy Yip*

Main category: math.NT

TL;DR: 证明了多个无理系数Beatty序列的质因数个数的联合分布收敛于多元标准高斯分布，并对单个Beatty序列给出了无条件的收敛速率界限。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 扩展经典的Erdős-Kac定理和Banks-Shparlinski在Beatty序列上的结果，研究多个Beatty序列的质因数个数的联合分布，并探讨收敛速率的无条件界限。

Method: 使用数论和概率论方法，分析多个无理系数的Beatty序列中质因数个数的分布，通过比较分布函数研究高斯收敛性及其收敛速率。

Result: 1) 证明了多个无理系数Beatty序列的质因数个数的联合分布收敛于多元标准高斯分布；2) 对单个Beatty序列证明了收敛速率为O((log log log N)/√(log log N))的无条件界限；3) 证明了这种通用界限在多元情况和高阶广义多项式下不存在。

Conclusion: 该工作将Erdős-Kac定理推广到多个Beatty序列的联合分布情况，并首次对单个Beatty序列给出了无需Diophantine假设的收敛速率界限，同时指出这种无条件界限无法扩展到多元情况和高阶多项式。

Abstract: The classical Erdős-Kac theorem states that for $n$ chosen uniformly at random from $1, \dots, N$, the random variable $(ω(n) - \log\log N)/\sqrt{\log\log N}$ converges in distribution to the standard Gaussian as $N$ tends to infinity. Banks and Shparlinski showed that this Gaussian convergence holds for any Beatty sequence $\lfloorαn + β\rfloor$ in place of $n$. Continuing in this spirit, Crnčević, Hernández, Rizk, Sereesuchart and Tao considered the joint distribution of $ω(n)$ and $ω(\lfloorαn\rfloor)$, which they showed to be asymptotically independent for irrational values of $α$.
  Generalising both results, we show that for any positive integer $k$, real numbers $α_1, \dots, α_k > 0$ and $β_1, \dots, β_k$, where $α_i/α_j$ is irrational for $i\neq j$, the joint distribution of $(ω(\lfloorα_in + β_i\rfloor) - \log\log N)/\sqrt{\log\log N}$ converges to the $k$-dimensional standard Gaussian.
  We next discuss quantitative bounds on the rate of convergence which do not depend on the values taken by the relevant parameters. Banks and Shparlinski remarked that such quantitative bounds may be given for a single Beatty sequence $\lfloorαn + β\rfloor$ under Diophantine type assumptions on $α$. We show that such assumptions are in fact unnecessary. Specifically, for any real numbers $α> 0, β$, we show that the Kolmogorov distance between the random variable $(ω(\lfloorαn + β\rfloor) - \log\log N)/\sqrt{\log\log N}$ and the standard Gaussian is bounded above by $O(\log\log\log N/\sqrt{\log\log N})$ as $N$ tends to infinity.
  On the other hand, we show that universal quantitative bounds of this kind do not exist for higher-degree generalised polynomials or for the joint convergence for multiple Beatty sequences.

</details>
