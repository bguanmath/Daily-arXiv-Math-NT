Method: 提出p-adic转移方法，使用纯非几何输入从一个空间提升Sturm型边界到另一个空间。具体将经典模形式的Sturm界转移到一级拟模形式空间。

Abstract: Sturm's theorem states that a modular form with coefficients in $\mathbb{Z}$ or $\mathbb{Z}/m\mathbb{Z}$ can only have an explicitly bounded order of vanishing at infinity. ### [5] [Eisenstein-prime Obstruction Sieve for Monogenicity](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.10488)
*Khai-Hoan Nguyen-Dang*

Main category: math.NT

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Alpöge--Bhargava--Shnidman发现即使加强的'无局部障碍'条件也不能保证全局幂整数基的存在，这引发了族层面问题：这种现象是否在单参数家族中持续存在？

Method: 研究纯数域K_m=ℚ(α)且α^n=m（n≥4）的情况，引入Eisenstein素数障碍筛法，通过抽象模板化的论证方法

Result: 证明满足g(m)>1但无ABS局部障碍的自由平方数m集合的自然密度为0，因此在纯数域族中，单生成性和α-单生成性具有相同的自然密度

Conclusion: Eisenstein素数障碍筛法可推广到其他Eisenstein参数族，提供了可重用的机制来解决这类数论问题

Abstract: Alpöge--Bhargava--Shnidman showed that even a strengthened \emph{no local obstruction} condition for monogenicity does not force a global power integral basis: in the full spaces of cubic and quartic fields, a positive proportion are non-monogenic yet satisfy this ABS fixed-sign condition. This raises a natural family-level question: does the same phenomenon persist inside one-parameter families, where the local structure varies in a highly constrained way?
  In this paper we answer this in the negative for the pure fields $K_m=\mathbb Q(α)$ with $α^n=m$ ($n\ge 4$) and $m$ square-free. Writing $g(m)=[\mathcal O_{K_m}:\mathbb Z[α]]$, we prove that the set of square-free $m$ for which $g(m)>1$ but $K_m$ has no ABS local obstruction has natural density $0$. Consequently, in the pure family monogenicity and $α$--monogenicity have the same natural density. The proof isolates a reusable mechanism, which we call the Eisenstein-prime obstruction sieve. The argument is packaged in an abstract template and transfers to other Eisenstein parameter families.

</details>
