*Victor Volfson*

Main category: math.NT

TL;DR: 本文研究整系数多元多项式产生素数值的渐近行为，基于概率启发式和多维Bateman-Horn猜想推导了计数的预期增长阶。重点关注奇异级数计算的精度，利用代数几何和解析数论方法获得奇异乘积尾部的估计。对于满足光滑条件的 一般情形建立了相对误差的上界；对于对角多项式，通过指数和分解和Katz公式在衰减指数和常数方面取得了改进。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究整系数多元多项式取素数值的渐近分布，检验Bateman-Horn猜想的预测，并提高奇异级数近似在计算实践中的精度。

Method: 综合运用代数几何和解析数论方法，包括奇异乘积尾部的估计、指数和的分解以及Katz公式。对于对角多项式，利用其因式分解结构获得更精细的结果。

Result: 建立了满足光滑条件的一般多元多项式相对误差的上界；对角多项式在衰减指数和常数方面取得改进，并提供展示了有效性的比较表格。

Abstract: This paper investigates the asymptotics of the number of prime values taken by a polynomial in several variables with integer coefficients. Based on probabilistic heuristics and the multidimensional Bateman Horn conjecture, the expected order of growth of this number is derived. The main focus is on the accuracy of computing the singular series. Using methods of algebraic geometry and analytic number theory, estimates for the tail of the singular product are obtained. For the general case of polynomials satisfying smoothness conditions, an upper bound for the relative error is established. For diagonal polynomials, due to the factorization of exponential sums and formula of Katz, an improvement is achieved both in the decay exponent and in the constant. Comparative tables are provided demonstrating the effectiveness of the diagonal case. The results make it possible to control the accuracy of the singular series approximation in practical computations and can be used for further progress towards the proof of the Bateman Horn conjecture for multivariate polynomials.

### [9] [On the automorphisms of the power semigroups of a numerical semigroup](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.26901)

<details>
