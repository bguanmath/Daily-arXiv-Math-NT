### [1] [On the discrete convolution of the Liouville and Möbius functions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.10241)

TL;DR: 本文研究刘维尔函数离散卷积S(n)的性质，使用CGZ方法得到加权平均的显式公式，进而获得S(n)的Dirichlet级数和幂级数的信息，以及多个λ因子卷积的信息。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究刘维尔函数的Goldbach类型计数函数S(n)的解析性质，探索其离散卷积行为，这是解析数论中重要的组合问题。

Method: 使用CGZ论文中引入的一般方法，针对满足特定条件的权重函数f(w)，推导S(n)加权平均的显式公式。

Result: 得到了加权平均的显式公式，该公式允许研究S(n)的Dirichlet级数和幂级数性质，并能推广到多个λ因子的离散卷积情形。

Conclusion: 通过显式公式成功刻画了S(n)的解析行为，为研究刘维尔函数的Goldbach类型表示提供了有力工具，并可推广到更一般的情形。

Abstract: In this article we study some properties of the discrete convolution of Liouville function $S(n):=\sum_{m_{1}+m_{2}=n}λ\left(m_{1}\right)λ\left(m_{2}\right)$, which is a Goldbach-type counting function of representations. In particular, using the general approach introduced in a recent paper \cite{CGZ}, we will give an explicit formula for weighted averages of $S(n)$ with a general weights $f(w)$ that verify suitable conditions. This formula allows us to obtain interesting information about the Dirichlet and power series of $S(n)$ and the discrete convolution with an arbitrary numbers of factors $λ(n)$.

### [2] [On the Existence of Integers with at Most 3 Prime Factors Between Every Pair of Consecutive Squares](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.10356)
*Peter Campbell*

Main category: math.NT

TL;DR: 本文证明了在区间$(n^2,(n+1)^2)$中至少存在一个最多只有3个素因子的整数，这改进了之前最多4个素因子的结果。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究Legendre猜想的几乎素数类似问题，即两个连续平方数之间的区间是否包含有限素因子的数，改善之前的最佳结果。

Method: 结合有限验证（计算$n^2 \\leq 10^{31}$时短区间内的素数以及最大素数间隔的显式边界）和筛法论证（对大n使用Richert对数权重和Bordignon等人的显式线性筛）。

Result: 成功证明了对于所有$n \\geq 1$，区间$(n^2,(n+1)^2)$中都存在一个最多只有3个素因子的整数（计重数）。

Conclusion: 该工作将Legendre猜想的几乎素数类似问题的结果从最多4个素因子改进到最多3个素因子，方法结合了计算验证和显式筛法理论。

Abstract: We prove an explicit almost-prime analogue of Legendre's conjecture. Namely, for every integer $n \geq 1$, the interval $(n^2,(n+1)^2)$ contains an integer having at most $3$ prime factors, counted with multiplicity. This improves the previous best result of Dudek and Johnston, who showed that every such interval contains an integer with at most $4$ prime factors. The proof combines a finite verification for $n^2 \leq 10^{31}$, obtained from computations on primes in short intervals between consecutive squares together with explicit bounds on maximal prime gaps, with a fully explicit sieve-theoretic argument for the remaining range. For large $n$, we adapt Richert's logarithmic weights to intervals between consecutive squares and employ an explicit linear sieve of Bordignon, Johnston, and Starichkova.

### [3] [Algebra Structures of Multiple Eisenstein Series in Positive Characteristic](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.10376)

Main category: math.NT

TL;DR: 本文证明了多重Eisenstein级数的一组线性无关性，建立了多重ζ值q-洗牌代数与多重Eisenstein级数空间之间的关系，验证了[CCHT25]中的代数结构猜想。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在[CCHT25]中引入了正特征域上的任意秩多重Eisenstein级数及其相关的q-洗牌代数，需要对这些对象的代数结构和相互关系进行深入研究。

Method: 通过建立多重ζ值的q-洗牌代数ℛ到多重Eisenstein级数空间逆极限的嵌入映射，以及证明多重Eisenstein级数的q-洗牌代数ℰ与ℛ的张量积同构。

Result: 1) 证明了多重Eisenstein级数的线性无关性；2) 建立了ℛ到多重Eisenstein级数空间逆极限的嵌入；3) 证明了ℰ ≅ ℛ ⊗ ℛ；4) 验证了ℰ是结合代数的猜想。

Conclusion: 多重Eisenstein级数的q-洗牌代数ℰ具有丰富的代数结构，它与多重ζ值的q-洗牌代数ℛ密切相关，这些结果为正特征域上的多重Eisenstein级数理论奠定了基础。

Abstract: In [CCHT25], the authors introduced multiple Eisenstein series of arbitrary rank in positive characteristic and the $q$-shuffle algebra $\mathcal{E}$ associated with them. In the present paper, we establish a class of linear independence results for multiple Eisenstein series. We also prove that the $q$-shuffle algebra $\mathcal{R}$ of multiple zeta values embeds into the inverse limit of the spaces of multiple Eisenstein series with respect to the rank $r$, and that $\mathcal{E}$ is isomorphic to the tensor square of $\mathcal{R}$. As an application, we show that $\mathcal{E}$ is an associative algebra, thereby verifying the conjecture proposed in [CCHT25]

### [4] [Transcendence of $p$-adic continued fractions and a quantitative $p$-adic Roth theorem](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.10561)

Main category: math.NT

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 改进p进连分式的超越性理论结果，特别是移除现有工作中对部分商或逼近的p进赋值的限制条件，为p进数域上的超越数论提供更一般的理论框架。

Method: 通过提供Ridout定量定理的版本（即p进版本的Roth定理），并研究代数数逼近中分母的增长情况，建立p进版本的Davenport和Roth的经典结果。

Result: 证明了回文和拟周期性p进连分式总是收敛于超越数或二次无理数，且此结论不依赖任何对p进范数的限制条件。

Conclusion: 该研究显著推进了p进连分式理论，特别是超越性结论的应用范围，并为p进数论提供了更强大的分析工具。

Abstract: In this paper, we improve some transcendence results for $p$--adic continued fractions. Moreover, we provide a quantitative version of Ridout's theorem (the $p$--adic analogue of Roth's theorem), and we study the growth of denominators of convergents of algebraic numbers, establishing a $p$--adic version of a well--known result of Davenport and Roth.
