### [1] [Lifting banal representations of classical groups](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.07516)
*Johannes Droschl*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 论文证明了在典型素数条件下，局部非阿基米德域上辛群和分裂正交群的不可约表示可从特征ℓ提升到特征0


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究特征ℓ表示理论的提升问题，特别关注典型素数情况下的表示提升性

Method: 通过分析群结构和典型素数条件，利用表示理论方法证明提升存在性

Result: 证明了对于每个典型素数ℓ，辛群或分裂正交群的任何光滑不可约表示都可以提升到特征0代数闭域上

Conclusion: 建立了典型素数条件下表示提升的普遍性，并将结果推广到其他经典群，应用于证明强典型情况下的Howe对偶性

Abstract: Let $\mathrm{G}$ be a symplectic or a split orthogonal group over a local non-archimedean field $\mathrm{F}$. A prime $\ell$ is called banal with respect to $\mathrm{G}$ if it does not divide the cardinality of the $k$-points of $\mathrm{G}$, where $k$ is the residue field of $\mathrm{F}$. In this paper we show that for every banal prime $\ell$, any smooth irreducible $\overline{\mathbb{F}}_\ell$-representation of $\mathrm{G}(\mathrm{F})$ admits a lift to $\overline{\mathbb{Q}}_\ell$. We also state similar results for more general classical groups of symplectic, orthogonal or unitary type. As an application we prove Howe-duality in the strongly banal case for symplectic-orthogonal or unitary dual pairs.

### [2] [L-modules are mixed](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.07719)
*Leslie Saper*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 证明了任意L-模都是混合的，即可以表示为不同层上的加权上同调L-模的迭代映射锥，并应用此结果证明了（排除某些类型外）$⁠widehat X$的交截上同调与加权上同调同构。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究局部对称空间X与其约化Borel-Serre紧化⁠widehat X上的L-模结构，旨在建立任意L-模的混合性质与构建新工具来分析拓扑不变量如交截上同调。

Method: 通过将任意L-模分解为不同层$X_P$上的加权上同调L-模的迭代映射锥，利用弱微支撑作为局部不变量来描述这种分解。证明了这些加权上同调模块可由不可约正则$L_P$-模V构造，且弱微支撑可计算地索引这些分解模块。

Result: 证明了任意L-模是混合的，即能表示为来自不同层$X_P$的加权上同调L-模的迭代映射锥。作为应用，证明了（排除D、E、F型）⁠widehat X的交截上同调同构于其加权上同调。

Conclusion: L-模的混合结构建立了一个统一框架，将其分解为可由弱微支撑索引的基本模块。该结果不仅提供了对L-模结构的深刻理解，还连接了交截上同调与加权上同调这两个重要拓扑不变量。

Abstract: Let X be the locally symmetric space associated to a reductive $\mathbb Q$-group G and an arithmetic subgroup $Γ$. An L-module M is a combinatorial model of a constructible complex of sheaves on $\widehat X$, the reductive Borel-Serre compactification of X whose strata $X_P$ are indexed by $Γ$-conjugacy classes of parabolic $\mathbb Q$-subgroups P of G. We show that any L-module M is "mixed" in the sense it is an iterated mapping cone of maps to or from shifted weighted cohomology L-modules on strata $X_P$ of $\widehat X$ with coefficients in V, an irreducible regular $L_P$-module. These weighted cohomology "building blocks" are indexed (up to multiplicity) by V in the weak micro-support of M which is a computable local invariant. As an application we prove that the intersection cohomology of $\widehat X$ is isomorphic to the weighted cohomology of $\widehat X$, at least excluding $\mathbb Q$-types D, E, and F.

### [3] [On Lower Bounds for sums of Fourier Coefficients of Twist-Inequivalent Newforms](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.07474)
*Moni Kumari,Prabhat Kumar Mishra,Jyotirmoy Sengupta*

Main category: math.NT

TL;DR: 研究两个不同扭形式f和g的傅里叶系数之和a_f(p)+a_g(p)的下界，给出了该和的最大质因子的下界估计及其在广义黎曼假设下的加强结果


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探究两个不同非CM正规化新形式傅里叶系数之和的性质，理解这类和的算术结构并建立有效的下界估计

Method: 采用筛法理论和素数分布技术，对a_f(p)+a_g(p)的最大质因子进行分析，在广义黎曼假设下获得更强结果，并应用Brun筛法推广到正整数集

Result: 证明对几乎所有素数p，a_f(p)+a_g(p)的最大质因子满足P(a_f(p)+a_g(p)) > (log p)^(1/14)(log log p)^(3/7-ε); 在广义黎曼假设下得到指数增长结果; 还得到若该和在正密度素数子集上较小，则f和g必须是二次特征扭等价的

Conclusion: 建立了两个不同非CM新形式傅里叶系数之和的精确下界估计，揭示了这类和的算术性质与扭等价性之间的深刻联系

Abstract: In this article, we address the lower bounds for the sums $a_f(p)+a_g(p)$ of the $p$-th Fourier coefficients of two twist-inequivalent, non-CM normalized newforms $f$ and $g$. Our main result shows that for such forms with integer Fourier coefficients, the largest prime factor of $a_f(p)+a_g(p)$ satisfies $P(a_f(p)+a_g(p)) > (\log p)^{1/14} (\log \log p)^{3/7-ε}$ for almost all primes $p$ and for any $ε> 0$. Beyond primes, we apply Brun's sieve to show that a similar phenomenon holds for a set of positive integers with natural density one. The main result is further strengthened under the Generalized Riemann Hypothesis, where we establish exponential growth for the absolute value of $a_f(p)+a_g(p)$ in terms of $p$.Additionally, we derive an interesting result related to the multiplicity one theorem, demonstrating that if the sum $a_f(p)+a_g(p)$ is small for a positive-density subset of primes, then $f$ and $g$ must be twist-equivalent by a quadratic character.
